## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
1. **Integration Complexity**
   - Mitigation: Phased rollout with fallback options

2. **LLM Reliability**
   - Mitigation: Human oversight at critical points

3. **Scalability Challenges**
   - Mitigation: Cloud-native architecture

### Organizational Risks
1. **Change Resistance**
   - Mitigation: Gradual adoption with training

2. **Skill Gap**
   - Mitigation: Comprehensive documentation and examples

3. **Resource Constraints**
   - Mitigation: Prioritized implementation
