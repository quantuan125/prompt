# PLAN: T104 (CWS) — Phase 1 / Stream `T104-PH001-ST005`: Template & Rules Alignment

## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Purpose**: Author and align the PLAN/ROADMAP/NOTES templates, authoring guidelines, and workspace documentation rules updates that codify the planning hierarchy standards (`T104-STD-001`) into reusable governance artifacts. These deliverables serve as: (a) authoring specs for LLM roles producing workspace artifacts, and (b) acceptance specs for `T104F (PLAN)` epic deliverables.

**Working draft caveat**: All deliverables produced by this stream are initial working drafts. Their details will be confirmed and finalized under epic `T104F (PLAN)` development after `T104-PH001`.
