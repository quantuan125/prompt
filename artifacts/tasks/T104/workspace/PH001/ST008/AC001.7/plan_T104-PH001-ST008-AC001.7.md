**Purpose**: Introduce the `comparative_analysis` subtype to the ANALYSIS artifact family by amending the analysis guideline (taxonomy, lifecycle position, frontmatter keys), adding a conditional template section with structured trade-study methodology (options enumeration, weighted evaluation criteria, scoring matrix, recommendation), and retroactively reclassifying the AC001.6 interim comparative assessment artifact.
