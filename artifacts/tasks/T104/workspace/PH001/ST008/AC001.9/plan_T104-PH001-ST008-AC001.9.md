# PLAN: T104 (CWS) - Phase 1 / Stream T104-PH001-ST008 / Sub-Activity AC001.9: Recyclable Loop Artifact Governance

## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Purpose**: Close the artifact-governance gaps exposed during AC001.6 multi-wave orchestration by codifying DEV-REPORT package taxonomy, VERIFICATION multi-report intake, sub-consultant traceability audit protocol, and recyclable-loop evidence handoff rules. This activity uses a two-gate model: consultation-only GATE-001 to approve the governance model, then implementation-backed GATE-002 to accept the resulting guideline/template amendments.
