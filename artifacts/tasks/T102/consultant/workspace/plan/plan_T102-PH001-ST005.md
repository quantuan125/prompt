# PLAN: T102 (CWD) — Phase 1 / Stream `T102-PH001-ST005`: Standards Amendment Execution (Research-Driven)

## I. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Purpose**: Receive integration recommendations from the `T102-PH001-ST004` research commissioning program and draft the actual STD clause amendments. This stream bridges the gap between "recommendations-only" research outputs and enforceable standard clauses.

**Scope**:
- In scope: Drafting clause-level amendments (new clauses + clause extensions) for standards identified by research integration packages. One Activity per standard being amended.
- Out of scope: Original research (ST004 scope), SSOT rollout of amended standards (downstream), and STD adoption/verification normalization (ST002 scope).

**Activity structure (per-STD, research-gated)**:
- `AC001`: Amend `T102-STD-007` (Issues & Risks Index) — **fully specified** from RES-004 Deltas 1-4

- `AC005`: Amend `T102-STD-005` (ID Specification & Rules) — **fully specified** from RES-005 Deltas C1-C2

**Coordination with ST002 (Sequencing Change)**: ST002 is now deferred until **ALL** ST005 GATE-001 approvals are recorded (DEC002 from `T102-PH001-SES001`). ST002-AC000 explicitly inventories ST005 outcomes to baseline the standards layer. ST005 proceeds as the primary standards amendment stream.

ST002-AC000 will inventory ST005 amendment outcomes as its first activity. ST005 amendments therefore serve as the primary input to ST002's post-amendment baselining process.

**Downstream conformance (hard gate for acceptance)**: Downstream artifacts (SPS/Concept/Request/templates) MAY be drafted in parallel, but MUST NOT be treated as "conformant" to the updated governance model until the relevant ST005 amended standards are approved (per-AC GATE-001). Any pre-ST005 drafting that touches affected surfaces MUST be revalidated post-ST005 and updated if needed.

**Non-goals**:
- This stream does not perform original research (ST004 produces that).
- This stream does not execute SSOT rollout of amended standards into SPS/Concept (downstream).
- This stream does not normalize adoption/verification contracts (ST002 scope).
