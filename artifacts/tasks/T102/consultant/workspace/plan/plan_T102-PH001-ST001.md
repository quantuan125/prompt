## II. STREAM DECISION SUMMARY (LOCKED)

Decisions below are treated as authoritative for Stream T102-PH001-ST001 planning as of **2026-02-04** (evidence: `T102-PH001-T102-PH001-ST001-AC001` analysis + activity notes).

1) **Where-spec-lives model**: **Model D (Combined ADR+Spec Files)** is authoritative for Phase 1.
2) **Execution strategy (Phase 1)**: **Combined ADR+Spec Files**:
   - Each ADR’s body and its `Specification/CLAUSE` content live in a **single dedicated file per ADR** (combined file).
   - Concept becomes the index-only hub; indexes point to combined files by **repo-relative canonical path**.
   - Index granularity is **the combined file as a whole** (no anchor addressing required in Phase 1).
3) **Canonical combined-file location (Phase 1)**: `prompt/artifacts/tasks/T102/consultant/standards/` (planned directory; created during rollout).
4) **Phase 1 stability contract**:
   - Combined-file **names/paths MUST NOT change** in Phase 1.
   - Combined files MUST use a clear internal structure boundary (recommended headings): `## Decision` / `## Specification`.
5) **Index authority**: the authoritative ADR indexes live in **Concept** (Concept as index hub).
6) **Concept scope (Phase 1 minimum)**: populate **ADR Index(es)** with canonical paths; other Concept navigation surfaces may remain skeleton-only in Phase 1.
7) **Phase 2 architectural target (deferred)**: optionally separate ADR vs spec into dedicated artifacts and/or evolve toward native `STD-...-CLAUSE-...` addressing once governance stabilizes.
8) **STD-contains-CLAUSE migration (Option C)**: Front-run Phase 2 target — CLAUSE reparented under `STD` as `STDCID`; golden exemplar rebuilt as `T102-STD-004`; section order reversed (`## Specification` first, `## Decision Record` second); alias window per CLAUSE-003B during transition; all other streams blocked until AC006 completes.
