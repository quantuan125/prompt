#### B. Narrative Summary (Minutes-Style)

The consultation expanded Phase 1 research commissioning beyond the initially registered `T102-RES-004 (Issues & Risks Architecture)` due to additional initiative-wide governance and architecture concerns that impact the standards system and SSOT placement strategy:

- Viability of the Explicit Inheritance Model (`T102-STD-003`) and observed “SPS bloat” from repeated Inherited Considerations sections.
- Viability of the Research Artifacts Index model (`T102-STD-006`) and whether Research indexing should remain in SPS/Request, move toward Concept, or be governed by stricter hybrid thresholds.
- Clarification of `T102C (CONCEPT)` as a dynamic SSOT registers surface and architectural description that supports SPS and Request, including any operating-model implications for `T102-STD-001`.

To keep research outputs decision-ready and to avoid a single mixed-scope report, the session confirmed these additional topics should be commissioned as separate initiative research IDs (`T102-RES-005/006/007`) while keeping `T102-RES-004` focused on Issues & Risks architecture.

The session also confirmed research outputs are **recommendations-only** (clause-level STD drafting is downstream), and that each commissioned research requires explicit gates for brief approval, report acceptance, and integration sign-off.

Per explicit client request, this Activity Notes file is created to capture the plan-amendment session even though AC001 has not yet transitioned to `in_progress` in the plan (exception to the just-in-time registration guideline).
